Use email app

This is the operating view of the app for staff and administrators. The current pilot sends staff notifications to david.chivers@durham.ac.uk during testing, but that destination can be changed later without rebuilding the workflow.

Step 1. Student submits the form

The student opens the Microsoft Form, signs in with their university account, completes the enquiry questions, and submits the message. The form records the responder identity automatically, so the student does not need to type programme, year, username, or email.

Form question guide

Step 2. The app creates a reference number

Power Automate creates a readable reference number such as DEPT-2026-000123. This should be used in follow-up communication.

Reference expression

Step 3. The app tries to enrich the enquiry

The flow uses the recorded responder email to check the Excel lookup table. That workbook should preferably be populated from Banner export or another trusted student-record source. If a match is found, the staff email includes student details such as student ID, programme, advisor, and Banner context. The app can also derive the CIS username from the email prefix if staff want that in the email.

Lookup schema

Step 4. Staff receive the enquiry by email

In the current pilot, david.chivers@durham.ac.uk receives one structured email for each enquiry. The subject line contains tags such as enquiry type, stage, year, and lookup status. Later, that same action can be repointed to another inbox or a shared mailbox.

Staff email template

Step 5. Student receives an acknowledgement

The student receives a short acknowledgement email confirming receipt and showing the reference number.

Student acknowledgement email

Step 6. Staff triage the enquiry in the mailbox

Version 1 stays email-first. Staff triage from the inbox. The app may suggest an owner, but it should not hard-route the enquiry automatically.

Step 7. If lookup fails, staff still work the enquiry

If no lookup match is found, the staff email should still be sent. Staff can use the recorded responder email and check Banner or the student record manually. The spreadsheet supports the flow, but it should not become the main staff working interface.

Step 8. Know the limits of version 1

This is not a case-management platform. It is a structured email intake and enrichment workflow. Keep it simple until the department has tested it properly, then move the mailbox destination if needed rather than redesigning the whole system.
